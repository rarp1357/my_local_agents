[LEVEL: A1 Movers]
[CORE_GOAL: Basic Communication]

[CAN_DO_STATEMENTS]
- Can understand simple descriptions, instructions, and narrations about familiar topics.
- Can talk about their day, their likes and dislikes, and simple activities using basic sentences.
- Can ask and answer simple questions and participate in short, structured interactions.

[LINGUISTIC_FOCUS_CONSTRAINTS]
- Prioritize: Present simple and continuous, past simple (regular verbs), basic comparatives, modal 'can', 'should', 'would like to'.
- Complexity Level: Low. Focus on connecting simple ideas with 'and', 'but', 'because'.
- Interaction Schema: Routine information exchange and simple narrative.
