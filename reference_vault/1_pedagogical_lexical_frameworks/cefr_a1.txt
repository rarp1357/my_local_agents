[CEFR_LEVEL: A1]
[CORE_GOAL: Breakthrough]

[CAN_DO_STATEMENTS]
- Can understand and use familiar everyday expressions and very basic phrases aimed at the satisfaction of needs of a concrete type.
- Can introduce themselves and others and can ask and answer questions about personal details such as where they live, people they know and things they have.
- Can interact in a simple way provided the other person talks slowly and clearly and is prepared to help.

[LINGUISTIC_FOCUS_CONSTRAINTS]
- Prioritize: Basic personal pronouns, present simple of 'to be' and 'have got', basic question words (who, what, where), common nouns for everyday objects.
- Complexity Level: Very low. Use short, simple sentences.
- Interaction Schema: Focus on concrete, immediate needs.
