[CEFR_LEVEL: A2]
[CORE_GOAL: Waystage]

[CAN_DO_STATEMENTS]
- Can understand sentences and frequently used expressions related to areas of most immediate relevance (e.g., very basic personal and family information, shopping, local geography, employment).
- Can communicate in simple and routine tasks requiring a simple and direct exchange of information on familiar and routine matters.
- Can describe in simple terms aspects of their background, immediate environment and matters in areas of immediate need.

[LINGUISTIC_FOCUS_CONSTRAINTS]
- Prioritize: Past simple, present continuous, basic modals of ability (can) and obligation (have to), simple comparative adjectives.
- Complexity Level: Low to moderate. Focus on routine matters.
- Interaction Schema: Routine information exchange.
