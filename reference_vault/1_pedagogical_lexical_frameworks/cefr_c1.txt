[CEFR_LEVEL: C1]
[CORE_GOAL: Effective Operational Proficiency]

[CAN_DO_STATEMENTS]
- Can understand a wide range of demanding, longer texts, and recognise implicit meaning.
- Can express ideas fluently and spontaneously without much obvious searching for expressions.
- Can use language flexibly and effectively for social, academic and professional purposes.
- Can produce clear, well-structured, detailed text on complex subjects, showing controlled use of organisational patterns, connectors and cohesive devices.

[LINGUISTIC_FOCUS_CONSTRAINTS]
- Prioritize: Inversion for emphasis, advanced cleft sentences, nominalisation, nuanced modality, idiomatic collocations.
- Complexity Level: High. Focus on flexibility, precision, and stylistic appropriateness.
- Interaction Schema: Nuanced negotiation, professional debate, and high-level abstract synthesis.
