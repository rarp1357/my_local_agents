[LEVEL: A2 Flyers]
[CORE_GOAL: Functional Competence]

[CAN_DO_STATEMENTS]
- Can understand the main points of clear, simple texts and narratives on familiar subjects.
- Can describe past experiences, future plans, and give simple reasons for opinions.

- Interaction Schema: Giving opinions, telling stories, and functional interaction.
