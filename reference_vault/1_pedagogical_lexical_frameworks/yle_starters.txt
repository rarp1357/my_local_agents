[LEVEL: Pre A1 Starters]
[CORE_GOAL: Introduction to English]

[CAN_DO_STATEMENTS]
- Can understand basic instructions, simple phrases, and everyday vocabulary related to familiar topics.
- Can answer simple questions about themselves, their home, and their surroundings.
- Can participate in very simple conversations if the other person speaks slowly and helps them.

[LINGUISTIC_FOCUS_CONSTRAINTS]
- Prioritize: Basic nouns (animals, food, school objects), present simple tense, 'to be', 'have got', simple prepositions of place.
- Complexity Level: Minimal. Use very short, concrete sentences.
- Interaction Schema: Basic questions and one-word or short-phrase responses.
